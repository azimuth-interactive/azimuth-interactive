Our Clients


Pearson Higher Education (Prentice Hall)

Azimuth PS authors have created multiple editions of a number of widely adopted textbooks for Pearson Higher Education, including E-commerce. Business. Technology. Society; Management Information Systems: Managing the Digital Firm; and Essentials of Management Information Systems. In addition, Azimuth PS performs the production work on these texts and creates all the supplementary educational and Web materials. Azimuth PS also created, managed and produced an 18-book and 12-CD series for Pearson Education titled The MCSE Microsoft Certification Series.

Kaplan Publishing

Azimuth PS works with Kaplan Publishing as project manager and production services provider for texts in various series of its renowned test preparation and textbook products, including MCAT, GRE, NCLEX and others.

McGraw-Hill

Azimuth PS created multiple editions of The Interactive Computing Series for McGraw-Hill, used by colleges and universities to teach Microsoft Office products. This series involved the creation of multiple books and accompanying interactive training CDs. Azimuth writers also created the introductory college text Computer Concepts for McGraw-Hill.

Cengage Learning Inc.

Azimuth PS writers created the widely adopted college text Information Technology and Society and accompanying interactive media, which was designed for use in liberal arts colleges and universities.

Pearson Custom Publishing

Azimuth PS works with Pearson Custom Publishing to re-design text for online delivery and develop lesson plans and learning strategies for proprietary schools and universities.

John Wiley

Azimuth PS worked with Wiley senior management to devise strategies for the adult learning market. Azimuth worked with Wiley editors to help create the Wiley Pathways Series in the Information Technology and Systems area. This Series involved the development of 12 books and related supplements aimed at the workforce education market. Azimuth recruited and vetted authors, coordinated the authoring process, and managed the pre-production process of integrating text and graphics.
